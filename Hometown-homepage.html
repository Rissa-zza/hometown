Top three activities to do at Cirebon

Batu lawang tourism

Visit kesepuhan palace

Go for a picnic at telaga nilam

Batu Lawang is a former cement mining area which has been transformed into an interesting and beautiful tourist attraction.
This palace has a museum that is quite complete and contains heirlooms and paintings from the royal collection.
Telaga nilam is a tourist spot that still has a natural feel,and it is a large lake that has lots of fish.
